Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 April 2026

DentaVet is a browser extension that provides interactive dental charting for veterinary patients. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Data Collection

DentaVet does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics with any external server, third party, or the extension developer.

Data Storage

All data created by the extension — including dental chart findings, patient names, and tooth mappings — is stored locally on your device using the Chrome browser's built-in storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your browser.

EzyVet Integration

When used alongside EzyVet, the extension interacts with the EzyVet web application within your browser. Specifically, it:

  • Reads dental chart information displayed on the EzyVet page you are viewing
  • Writes dental findings into EzyVet's dental record forms on your behalf
  • Fetches the dental chart diagram image from your EzyVet session for report generation

All of these interactions occur within your existing, authenticated EzyVet session. The extension does not access EzyVet's servers independently, does not store your EzyVet credentials, and does not transmit any EzyVet data externally.

Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • Side Panel — to display the dental charting interface
  • Storage — to save chart data locally on your device
  • Active Tab — to interact with the EzyVet page you are viewing
  • Scripting — to read and write dental data on the EzyVet page
  • Host access (*.ezyvet.com) — to enable integration with EzyVet pages only

Third-Party Services

The extension uses the LemonSqueezy API solely for licence key activation and validation. Only your licence key is transmitted to this service for the purpose of verifying your subscription. No patient data, clinical findings, dental chart information, or personally identifiable information is ever sent to LemonSqueezy or any other third party.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with anyone. The extension developer has no access to any data you create or view using the extension.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Significant changes will be noted in the extension's changelog.
