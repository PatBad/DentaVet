One-time setup

Before you can push findings to EzyVet, you need to map the teeth in the dental chart. Open any patient's dental chart in EzyVet, then click Start Mapping below. Click each tooth number in the panel, then click its polygon in the chart. This only needs to be done once per species.

For each tooth with findings, the extension will click it in EzyVet, tick the correct checkboxes (PD, TR, fracture, etc.) and fill in probing depths — automatically.
